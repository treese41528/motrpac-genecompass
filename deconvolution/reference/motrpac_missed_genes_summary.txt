MoTrPAC training-regulated ('primary') genes NOT tokenizable after the bulk gene-ID liftover
=============================================================================================

534 of the 9,800 training-regulated transcriptome genes remain without a GeneCompass
token even after the liftover (8,768 already in vocab + 498 recovered by the symbol bridge
= 9,266 covered; these 534 are the residual). Flagged by importance (n_tissues + curation).

MHC (RT1-*) genes are training-regulated immune genes (paper emphasizes immune trajectories)
but are hyper-polymorphic and excluded from ortholog-based vocabularies.

IMPORTANT CAVEAT on 'can we recover these by growing the vocab?':
  Determining whether each missed gene occurs in our single-cell corpus (the prerequisite
  for minting a GeneCompass token) could NOT be settled locally: the biomart rat_gene_info
  Gene-name column is empty for 43% of rat genes (it lacks even ACTB/CD36), so symbol->
  current-ENSRNOG resolution is unreliable. A proper rat symbol map (RGD) or an Ensembl
  stable-ID HISTORY converter (Rnor_6.0 -> mRatBN7.2) is needed to resolve SC presence and
  to attempt a third (ID-history) liftover bridge. Deferred per user.
